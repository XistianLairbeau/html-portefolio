The Best Movies According to Christian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kill Bill

A really high-paced and intense Kung-Fu/Western inspired movie. It follows a world-class assasin, seeking revenge on a treachorous gang of colleagues. In my opinion this is Quentin Tarantino at his best.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy.

Based on a litterary masterpiece by J.R.R. Tolkien, the movies are Peter Jacksons' cinematic masterpiece. The movies stay incredibly true to the books for the most part and the cast is incredible.

The Matrix

The original Matrix movie is an Action/Sci-Fi movie that manages to tell an enticing story with lots of action as well as deep philosophical themes weaved into the plot. Perhaps the most action-filled introduction to philosophy there is.
